1. GETTING STARTED
================================

What's in the Box
-----------------
□ SmartWatch Pro X1
□ Charging cable (USB-C)
□ Quick start guide
□ Warranty card
□ Two additional band sizes (S and L)

If any items are missing, please contact customer support within 30 days of purchase.

Initial Setup
-------------

Step 1: Charge Your Watch
Before first use, charge your SmartWatch Pro X1 for at least 30 minutes.
- Connect the charging cable to the watch
- LED indicator will turn orange during charging
- LED turns green when fully charged

Step 2: Power On
Press and hold the side button for 3 seconds until the screen illuminates.

Step 3: Select Language
- Swipe left or right to scroll through language options
- Tap your preferred language to select
- Supported languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean

Step 4: Download the Companion App
- Scan the QR code displayed on your watch
- Or search "SmartWatch Pro" in App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
- Download and install the free app

Step 5: Pair Your Watch
On your smartphone:
1. Open the SmartWatch Pro app
2. Tap "Add New Device"
3. Select "SmartWatch Pro X1"
4. Follow on-screen pairing instructions
5. Grant necessary permissions (Bluetooth, Location, Notifications)

The watch will display a 6-digit code. Confirm this code matches the one shown on your phone.

Step 6: Complete Profile
Enter your personal information for accurate fitness tracking:
- Age
- Height
- Weight
- Gender
- Activity level

This information is stored locally and encrypted for your privacy.

First Time Tips
---------------
- Take 5 minutes to explore the main menu
- Swipe up for notifications, down for quick settings
- Press the side button to return to watch face
- Long press the watch face to customize it

3. HEALTH AND FITNESS FEATURES
================================

Heart Rate Monitoring
---------------------

Continuous Heart Rate:
Your watch automatically measures heart rate every 5 minutes throughout the day.

Manual Measurement:
1. Open the Heart Rate app
2. Keep your wrist still
3. Wait 10-15 seconds for reading
4. View current, resting, and daily average heart rate

Heart Rate Zones:
- Resting: < 60 bpm
- Fat Burn: 60-70% of max
- Cardio: 70-85% of max
- Peak: 85-100% of max

High/Low Heart Rate Alerts:
Enable alerts for abnormal heart rates:
- Settings > Health > Heart Rate Alerts
- Set threshold for high heart rate (e.g., >120 bpm when inactive)
- Set threshold for low heart rate (e.g., <40 bpm)

Sleep Tracking
--------------

Automatic Sleep Detection:
The watch automatically detects when you fall asleep and wake up.

Sleep Metrics:
- Total sleep time
- Sleep stages (Light, Deep, REM)
- Time to fall asleep
- Number of times awakened
- Sleep quality score (0-100)

Improving Sleep Tracking Accuracy:
- Wear watch snug but comfortable
- Enable sleep mode for improved battery life
- Set consistent bedtime schedule
- Keep firmware updated

Viewing Sleep Data:
- On watch: Open Sleep app
- On phone: More detailed charts and trends in companion app
- Weekly and monthly summaries available

Activity Tracking
-----------------

Daily Activity Goals:
- Steps: Default 10,000 (customizable)
- Active minutes: Default 30
- Calories burned: Personalized based on profile
- Standing hours: Default 12

The watch vibrates when you've been inactive for 50 minutes during waking hours.

Auto-Detected Activities:
The watch automatically recognizes:
- Walking (10+ minutes)
- Running (5+ minutes)
- Cycling (10+ minutes)
- Swimming (5+ minutes)

Workout Modes
-------------

Starting a Workout:
1. Press side button
2. Scroll to Workout app
3. Select activity type
4. Set goals (optional): duration, distance, or calories
5. Tap "Start"
6. Workout begins after 3-second countdown

Available Workout Modes:
- Outdoor Running
- Indoor Running (treadmill)
- Outdoor Cycling
- Indoor Cycling
- Swimming (pool)
- Open Water Swimming
- Walking
- Hiking
- Yoga
- Strength Training
- HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
- Elliptical
- Rowing
- Dance
- Pilates
- Custom (create your own)

During Workout:
- Swipe right to pause/resume
- Swipe left to end workout
- Real-time metrics displayed:
  * Duration
  * Distance (GPS-based)
  * Pace/Speed
  * Heart rate
  * Calories burned
  * Heart rate zone

Post-Workout Summary:
After ending workout, view:
- Complete statistics
- Route map (for GPS activities)
- Heart rate chart
- Pace graph
- Personal records achieved

GPS Tracking:
- Standalone GPS - no phone needed
- GPS + GLONASS for improved accuracy
- Allow 30-60 seconds for GPS lock before starting outdoor activities

Water Lock Mode:
For swimming and water activities:
1. Swipe down for quick settings
2. Tap water drop icon
3. Touchscreen is disabled to prevent accidental touches
4. Rotate digital crown to eject water after swimming

SpO2 (Blood Oxygen) Monitoring
-------------------------------

Spot Check:
1. Open SpO2 app
2. Keep wrist still and elevated to heart level
3. Wait 15 seconds
4. View reading (normal range: 95-100%)

Overnight Tracking:
Enable in Settings > Health > SpO2 Monitoring
- Automatically measures during sleep
- Helps identify potential breathing issues
- Drains approximately 5% additional battery

Stress Monitoring
-----------------

The watch uses heart rate variability to estimate stress levels:
- Low (0-25): Relaxed state
- Medium (26-50): Normal daily stress
- High (51-75): Elevated stress
- Very High (76-100): Significant stress

Breathing Exercises:
When stress is detected as high:
1. Watch suggests breathing exercise
2. Follow on-screen animations
3. Inhale and exhale guided by expanding/contracting circle
4. Default session: 2 minutes (customizable 1-5 minutes)

Menstrual Cycle Tracking
-------------------------

Setup:
1. Open Women's Health app
2. Enter last period start date
3. Set average cycle length
4. Set average period length

Features:
- Period predictions
- Fertile window estimates
- Log symptoms and flow
- Reminders for upcoming periods
- Privacy protected - data encrypted

4. CONNECTIVITY AND APPS
================================

Bluetooth Connection
--------------------

Pairing Range: Up to 10 meters (33 feet)

Connection Status Icons:
- Blue Bluetooth icon: Connected to phone
- Gray Bluetooth icon: Disconnected
- No icon: Bluetooth disabled

Reconnecting:
If connection drops:
1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on phone
2. Keep watch and phone within range
3. Open companion app on phone
4. Connection restores automatically

Unpair/Forget:
To unpair watch:
- Settings > Connectivity > Forget This Watch
- Note: This erases all data on the watch

Wi-Fi
-----

Connecting to Wi-Fi:
1. Settings > Connectivity > Wi-Fi
2. Toggle Wi-Fi on
3. Select network
4. Enter password using on-screen keyboard

Wi-Fi enables:
- Firmware updates
- Music streaming
- App updates
- Voice assistant queries
- Notification sync when phone is not nearby

Supported Standards: 802.11 b/g/n (2.4 GHz)

Phone Calls
-----------

Answering Calls:
When phone rings:
- Tap green phone icon to answer
- Tap red phone icon to decline
- Swipe left for quick response messages

During Call:
- Built-in microphone and speaker allow hands-free conversation
- Adjust volume using digital crown
- Tap "Transfer to Phone" to move call to phone
- Maximum call duration: No limit while connected

Making Calls:
- Use voice assistant: "Call [contact name]"
- Browse contacts: Contacts app
- Call recent numbers: Phone app > Recent

Note: Watch must be connected to phone for calls

Music Control

Control Phone Music:

Standalone Music (Premium Feature):

Voice Assistant

Activation:

Voice Commands:

Supported Assistants:

NFC Payments
------------

Setup:
1. Open Wallet app on phone
2. Add credit/debit cards
3. Verify with bank
4. Cards sync to watch automatically

Making a Payment:
1. Double-press side button to open Wallet
2. Select card
3. Hold watch near payment terminal
4. Wait for confirmation vibration
5. No PIN needed for transactions under $50 (varies by region)

Security:
- Cards are tokenized (actual numbers not stored)
- Payment requires watch to be unlocked
- Auto-locks when removed from wrist

10. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)
================================

Q: Is the watch waterproof?
A: Yes, water-resistant up to 50 meters (5 ATM). Suitable for swimming, showering, and water sports. Not suitable for scuba diving or high-pressure water activities.

Q: How accurate is the heart rate monitor?
A: Clinical studies show 95% accuracy compared to chest strap monitors during steady-state activities. Accuracy may decrease during high-intensity interval training.

Q: Can I replace the watch band?
A: Yes, the watch uses standard 20mm quick-release bands. Any 20mm band will work.

Q: How do I clean my watch?
A: Wipe with slightly damp cloth. For bands, use mild soap and water. Dry thoroughly before wearing. Do not use harsh chemicals or abrasives.

Q: What do I do if my watch won't turn on?
A: 1) Charge for at least 30 minutes, 2) Try forced restart by holding side button for 15 seconds, 3) Contact support if issue persists.

Q: Can I use the watch without a smartphone?
A: Yes, many features work standalone (time, fitness tracking, music playback, GPS). However, initial setup and some features require a phone.

Q: Is my health data private?
A: Yes, all health data is encrypted and stored locally. You control what information is shared. See Privacy Policy for details.

For additional questions, visit support.smartwatchpro.com or contact customer service.
