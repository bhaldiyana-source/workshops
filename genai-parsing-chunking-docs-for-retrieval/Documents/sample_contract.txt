(a) Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within the notice period;

Licensee may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days' written notice, provided that:

(a) Licensee pays all fees through the end of the then-current term;

(b) Licensee's use poses a security risk or violates applicable laws;

(a) All licenses granted hereunder shall immediately terminate;
(b) Licensee shall cease all use of the Licensed Software and Documentation;
